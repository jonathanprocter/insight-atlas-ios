## Key Design Principles

1. **Classical Elegance:** Serif typography with generous line-height (1.75)
2. **Warm Tones:** Sepia and cream backgrounds for approachability
3. **Gold Accents:** Used for emphasis and brand identity
4. **Rounded Corners:** 12px radius for modern, friendly feel
5. **Generous Spacing:** 24px padding in boxes for readability
6. **Icon Integration:** Emoji icons for visual interest and quick identification
